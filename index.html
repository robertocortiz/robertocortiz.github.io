Instructions for Jarvis Home Automation

To begin using Jarvis:

• Please click on any room on the Control Plane, this will bring up the menu with the room selected on the Left.

• Once the Menu is available, You can toggle ON toggle_on or OFF toggle_off the Light and Curtains

• You can also set the desired room temperature in Fahrenheit

• If you would like to control multiple rooms just select the Menu on the top left hand corner to view all rooms
